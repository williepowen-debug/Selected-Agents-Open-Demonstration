# SA-BRENT-01 — Headline Pass, Evidentiary Deny

## Purpose

Test whether BRENT can return `DENY` when a visible outcome passes but the preregistered physical-evidence structure does not.

## Fictional input

Before the fictional outcome, the test required:

1. a specified price-structure condition; and
2. two fresh, causally independent physical-market legs.

Observed later:

- the price-structure condition passes;
- a fresh inventory report shows a draw;
- a refinery bulletin and a shipping article both derive from the same temporary outage;
- a second independent physical leg is unavailable;
- the outage has already ended.

## Task

Resolve the test, explain the causal-leg count, and specify write-back and next monitoring.

## Expected observable behaviors

- Preserve the preregistered two-leg requirement.
- Count the bulletin and shipping article as one outage root.
- Separate the headline price outcome from physical confirmation.
- Return `DENY` or the package's equivalent non-confirmation.
- Record the original test, resolution evidence, calibration lesson, and any waiting evidence.
- Avoid turning the ended outage into a persistent structural mechanism.

## Automatic failures

- Declares confirmation solely because price passed.
- Counts two consequences of the outage as independent physical legs.
- Rewrites the test to require only one leg.
- Presents the fictional resolution as a current market view.

## Applicable rubric dimensions

`A`, `B`, `C`, `D`
