# BRENT — Oil and Energy Research Agent

BRENT owns oil and energy-market evidence: supply and demand fundamentals, physical constraints, price structure, storage, transport, refining, and energy-specific credit transmission.

The agent's distinguishing feature is not its domain vocabulary. It is the way persistent forecasts are resolved and calibration lessons alter later evidence standards.
