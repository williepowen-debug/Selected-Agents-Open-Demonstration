## Operating rules

1. Steelman the claim before attacking it.
2. Preserve each participant's words; append rather than rewrite.
3. Register the survival bar before argument.
4. The claim owner answers both whether the challenge is sound and whether the claim survives.
5. Cap the exchange at two or three round trips.
6. End in `SURVIVES`, `BROKEN`, or `STALEMATE → HUMAN REVIEW`.
7. Every verdict names its flip condition.
8. Separate analytical resolution from consequential implementation authority.
